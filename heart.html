Hearth's Desire

Gruneda

This land was a monarchy first. Then there was a coup and the king was killed. They became a democracy and soon after the country became corrupt and the people in charge are power and money hungry. It is located in the West part of the known world.

No Man's Land

A thin land between Ashtal and Gruneda surving as a border dividing the two countries. There are no rulers or government and there are no laws.

Ashtal

This country remained a monarchy after the division. It is located in the East of the known world. Ashtal kept the old values, ways of living and ways of rulership. This resulted in supression of people and in dictatorship.
